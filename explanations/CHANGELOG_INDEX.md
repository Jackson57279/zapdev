## Semantic Versioning

ZapDev follows Semantic Versioning (MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH):

- **MAJOR** - Breaking changes, significant rewrites
- **MINOR** - New features, enhancements (backward compatible)
- **PATCH** - Bug fixes, security patches (backward compatible)
